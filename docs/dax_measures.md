# DAX Measures Summary

This document summarises the main DAX logic used in the Energy Flow Dashboard.

## Key Measure Groups

### Energy Consumption Measures

- Total renewable energy
- Total non-renewable energy
- Total energy consumption

### Cost Measures

- Total energy cost
- Cost per kilowatt hour
- Cost comparison by energy type

### Emissions Measures

- Emission factor
- Emissions-related KPI calculations

### Year-over-Year Measures

- Previous year energy value
- Current year energy value
- Year-over-year percentage change
- Conditional formatting logic for positive and negative changes

### Dynamic Dashboard Measures

- Field parameter-based metric selection
- Dynamic chart titles
- Dynamic label formatting
- Conditional highlighting for selected metrics

## Skills Demonstrated

- Explicit DAX measures
- Dynamic metric selection
- KPI calculation
- Percentage share calculations
- Conditional formatting
- Text-based summary measures
- Time-based trend analysis
