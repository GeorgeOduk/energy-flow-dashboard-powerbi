# Energy Flow Dashboard Project Summary

## Project Objective

The objective of this project was to build an interactive Power BI dashboard that analyses energy consumption, cost efficiency and emissions across selected locations.

The dashboard focuses on comparing renewable and non-renewable energy usage while highlighting trends over time, seasonal patterns and cost differences between energy types.

## Locations Analysed

The dashboard focuses on:

- Atlanta
- Chicago
- New York

## Key Analytical Areas

### 1. Energy Consumption

The dashboard tracks total energy consumption and allows users to compare renewable, non-renewable and total energy usage.

### 2. Renewable vs Non-Renewable Energy

The report compares renewable and non-renewable energy usage to show the energy mix across the selected locations.

### 3. Cost Efficiency

The dashboard analyses energy costs and shows how cost patterns differ by energy type and over time.

### 4. Emissions

The report includes emissions-related metrics to support analysis of environmental impact.

### 5. Trend Analysis

The dashboard highlights changes over time, including monthly trends and year-over-year comparisons.

### 6. Seasonality

Seasonal analysis was included to show how energy consumption changes across winter, spring, summer and fall.
